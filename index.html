My Story

A Beacon of Light
on Your Journey

I am Sahibaa Singh — a life coach, mentor, and guide who believes every person holds within them an extraordinary capacity for growth, resilience, and joy. My work is rooted in the belief that clarity is the greatest gift you can give yourself.

"When we stop being afraid of our own light, we become the lighthouse for others."

With over a decade of experience working with professionals, executives, entrepreneurs, and individuals at crossroads, I bring a deeply personal, evidence-based approach to life coaching that honours both the mind and the heart.
